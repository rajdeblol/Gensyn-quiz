10 key hard facts about Pharos Network

1. What is Pharos Network?

Pharos is a full-stack parallel Layer-1 blockchain, EVM-compatible, designed for high-speed scalable dApps, bridging $50T+ in RWAs, TradFi, and cross-chain liquidity into a modular on-chain economy at internet scale.

2. What is its core technology?

Features Pharos VM (Dora) with parallel execution, superscalar pipelining, unified IR for EVM/WebAssembly, hyper-parallelism in consensus/execution/storage, and optimizations reducing storage by 80% via GPU-inspired design.

3. What performance does it achieve?

50,000+ TPS with sub-second finality; Testnet processed 2.24B+ transactions across 155M+ unique addresses, average block time 0.2s, live for 100+ days.

4. Who founded Pharos?

Founded in 2024 by ex-Ant Financial/Alibaba leaders: CEO Alex Zhang (former CTO of Ant Chain), CTO Meng Wu (Chief Security Officer for Ant Financial's Web3 division), and co-founders including Zack Yang and Wish Wu.

5. What funding has it raised?

$8M seed round closed Sept 2024 as SAFE with token warrants; co-led by Lightspeed Faction & Hack VC, with SNZ Capital (anchor), Reforge, Dispersion Capital, Hash Global, Generative Ventures, Legend Star, MH Ventures, Zion, Chorus One Ventures.

6. What are its key use cases?

On-chain CLOB DEX, real-time payments, institution-level RWAs, scalable DePIN; supports heterogeneous hardware/software, decentralized AI, privacy tech for high-throughput DeFi and enterprise finance.

7. What partnerships does it have?

Strategic partnership with ZAN (Ant Digital Technologies' Web3 brand) for node service, security, hardware acceleration; integrations: Orbiter Finance (ETH bridging), Morpho (DeFi lending), Bitverse (AI RWA PerpDEX).

8. What is its team size and locations?

11-50 employees (<10 core as of Nov 2024); HQ in Road Town, Tortola (BVI); operations in U.S., Hong Kong, Singapore; plans to expand to 20+ in cryptography, marketing, ecosystem development.

9. What is the roadmap?

Testnet live (Q1 2025+); Mainnet H2 2025; focus on enhancing Testnet, RWAfi partnerships, "Real Assets in Motion" stack for real yield/on-chain trading, cross-chain liquidity at internet scale.

10. What are its achievements?

Ecosystem: 94K+ new Discord members (total ~900K); events: HKFintechWeek2025, Asia Stablecoin Conference 2025 sponsor/speaker, TOKEN2049 RWA side events; drives mass adoption via efficient RWA/DeFi integration.
